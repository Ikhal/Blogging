What are Circuit Boards

A circuit is a closed loop or pathway that allows electrical current to flow, facilitating the transfer of electric energy. It comprises components such as resistors, capacitors, inductors, and semiconductors, interconnected by conductive wires. Circuits can be simple, like those found in household appliances, or complex, as seen in electronic devices. The arrangement and combination of circuit elements determine its functionality, enabling tasks such as amplification, signal processing, or computation. Understanding circuits is fundamental to electronics, as they form the backbone of electronic systems, providing the structure for the controlled flow and manipulation of electrical signals..
